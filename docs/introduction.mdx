## Contrax Summary

Welcome to the simplest way to earn high returns on your crypto! Contrax is a web app that allows you to easily buy, stake, and earn on your crypto in a few clicks.
You don't have to wrestle with figuring out wallets, gas, LP tokens, swapping rewards, or anything complex. Just buy, stake, and earn!

Contrax is fully non-custodial, so you'll always fully cotrol your funds. We never hold or have any control over them.
